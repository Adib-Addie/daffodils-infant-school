Who We Are

Our Story

Welcome to Daffodils Infant School, where education meets innovation and care. We are dedicated to creating a nurturing environment that inspires young minds to explore, learn, and grow with confidence.

Our modern approach combines traditional values with contemporary teaching methods, ensuring every child receives personalized attention and develops at their own pace. We believe in making learning fun, interactive, and meaningful.

With experienced teachers, state-of-the-art facilities, and a child-centered curriculum, we are committed to building strong foundations for lifelong success. Join us in shaping tomorrow's leaders today.

Our Mission

With 25 years of excellence in early childhood education, we nurture young minds and build strong foundations for lifelong learning. Our joyful, stimulating environment ensures every child feels valued, challenged, and inspired to reach their full potential.

To be a leading institution in early childhood education, recognized for our innovative teaching methods, holistic development approach, and commitment to creating confident, compassionate, and capable future leaders.

Milestones

Our Journey

A proud timeline of growth, achievement, and dedication to young learners

1999
The Beginning

Daffodils Infant School was founded with a vision to provide quality early childhood education in Patna, Bihar, starting with just a handful of students.

2005
New Campus

Expanded to a larger, purpose-built campus with modern classrooms, a play zone, and dedicated activity rooms to serve our growing student community.

2012
Recognition & Awards

Received the State Award for Excellence in Early Childhood Education, recognizing our innovative curriculum and outstanding student outcomes.

2018
Digital Learning

Introduced smart classrooms and digital learning tools, blending traditional values with modern technology to prepare students for the future.

2024
25 Years of Excellence

Celebrating 25 glorious years of shaping young minds, with over 5,000 successful alumni and a continued commitment to nurturing the next generation.
