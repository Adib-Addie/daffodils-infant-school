How We Teach

Teaching Methods

Multiple Intelligence Approach — recognising and nurturing every type of learner

Play-Based Learning

Engages bodily-kinesthetic and interpersonal intelligence through structured play, games, and group interactions that make learning genuinely fun and deeply memorable for every child.

Logical-Mathematical Development

Develops logical-mathematical intelligence through puzzles, problem-solving activities, math games, and scientific experiments that build strong critical thinking skills from an early age.

Musical & Spatial Learning

Nurtures musical and spatial intelligence through music activities, art projects, 3D modeling, and creative expression that enhances artistic thinking and visual reasoning capabilities.

Linguistic & Intrapersonal Growth

Develops linguistic intelligence through storytelling, vocabulary building, reading, writing, and poetry — while nurturing intrapersonal intelligence through journaling, self-reflection, and emotional awareness practices.
