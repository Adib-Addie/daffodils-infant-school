Step by Step

Admission Process

Simple, transparent and stress-free enrollment in just five easy steps

1
Inquiry & Visit

Visit our school campus to understand our facilities, teaching methods, and environment. Meet our staff and get a feel of the warm learning atmosphere.

2
Application Form

Fill out the admission application form with accurate information. Provide all required details about your child and family background carefully.

3
Document Submission

Submit all required documents including birth certificate, immunization records, parent's ID proof, and passport-size photographs.

4
Interaction & Assessment

A friendly interaction session with your child to assess readiness, followed by a parent discussion to understand expectations and answer queries.

5
Admission Confirmation

Receive your admission offer, complete fee payment, and get your child enrolled. Welcome to the Daffodils family — we can't wait to meet them!

Age is calculated from the birth certificate. The cutoff date for age calculation is 31st March of the admission year. Original documents must be presented at the time of admission.

What to Bring

Required Documents

Keep these documents ready for a smooth and hassle-free admission process

Essential Documents
  • Birth Certificate
    Original and one photocopy required
  • Passport Size Photographs
    4 recent photographs of the child
  • Parent's ID Proof
    Aadhar card or voter ID (both parents)
  • Immunization Records
    Complete up-to-date vaccination record
Additional Documents
  • Aadhar Card
    Child's Aadhar card (if available)
  • Medical Fitness Certificate
    Issued by a registered doctor
  • Previous School Report
    If applicable (Classes 2 and above)
  • Address Proof
    Ration card or recent utility bill
